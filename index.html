Color description

A description of primary and secondary colors used in this theme as a main brand colors. And additional UI colors, such as colors for backgrounds, surfaces, errors, typography, and iconography.

Background colors

  • Primary surface and backgroud color for scrollable content is white.
  • For contrast with primary surface color is used black color.
  • Surface and backgroud colors don't represent the brand or style of the site

Card

Secondary surface color appears in cards, sheets and menu.

Primary colors

  • Primary colors are most frequently displayed colors on the page
  • It usualy represents the brand colors.
  • Primary colors are used for separate lines, primary buttons and headings backgrounds and other decorations.
  • There is also a bright variant of the colors, which can appear as a hover or to make a contrast between two elements.
